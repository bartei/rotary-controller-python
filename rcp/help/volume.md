Sound Volume
============

Controls the volume level for audible feedback (beeps) when the
DRO reaches a target position or confirms an action.

## Usage

- Drag the slider to adjust volume (0–100%)
- Press "Test" to hear a sample beep at the current volume
- Set to 0 to mute all sounds

## Notes

- The sound plays through the system's default audio output
- In a noisy workshop, higher volumes help confirm operations
  without looking at the screen
